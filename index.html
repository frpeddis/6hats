No API key. No money. Uses your existing Claude.ai account.

Step by step:

  1. Generate the prompt — the app builds a deep six-hats prompt from your topic and shows it for review
  2. Copy prompt — when you're happy with what was generated, click this on the prompt panel
  3. Open Claude.ai — click this to open Claude in a new tab. Paste with ⌘V / Ctrl+V, press Enter, wait for the six perspectives
  4. Copy Claude's full reply and paste it back here — the app splits it into the six cards and gives each character a voice
What gets shared: only the prompt you generate. It goes to your clipboard and (if you click in Claude) to your own Claude.ai session. The app never sends anything anywhere on its own. Nothing is stored.
One-time setup, about 60 seconds:

  2. Click "Create API key" → "Create API key in new project"
  3. Copy the key (it starts with AIzaSy…) and paste it below

Manual voice mapping
Your key can't list voices automatically (missing voices_read scope). You can either regenerate the key with that scope, or assign voice IDs manually here.

How to get voice IDs: go to elevenlabs.io → My Voices, click any voice you've added, then click the three-dot menu → Copy voice ID. Paste one into each box below. Leave blank to fall back to the browser voice for that hat.
